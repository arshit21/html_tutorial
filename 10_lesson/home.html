When did tacos first appear inthe United States?

Tacos were first introduced to the United States in 1905, when Mexican migrants brought the food with them to work on railroads and in mines. At first, tacos were considered a lower-class food, but as the children of migrant workers grew up and became more economically advantaged, tacos became mainstream
